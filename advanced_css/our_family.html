Champion's Dad and Sister

In October of 2019 Champion and I grew our family of 2 to a family of 3. Champion and I were out on a walk when he actually picked my fiance, Robert, to become his friend. Over the years we have bonded and grew to do everything together. Then in July of 2024 we welcomed our beautiful baby girl Epiphany into the world. Champion gained a new title of big brother/ security guard. Now almost two years later they are inseperable. Champion and Epiphany play all day long, they spend every meal together, and if out of each other site for even 5 minutes they are both upset. Champion has helped me to build the best things I could have ever hoped for, a loving family.

Our Family Traditions

Eventhough our family is fairly new we have built so many traditions. For all holidays we make sure to have matching outfits that include Champion. He enjoys some of them but he more enjoys the thought of food from whatever holiday we are celebrating. We also are keeping our tradition going that we started before having Epiphany, we like to take road trips twice a year. We always include Champion because he loves being outdoors and being in nature. His favorite two places are polar opposites. He loves the beach and the snow, anything he can dig into he loves. I believe this summer he will have Epiphany in tow as they both dig in the sand.
